About Me

I am doing my PhD in Bioinformatics from University of Copenhagen in Denmark. In my thesis, I develop statistical methods to analyse data from trilions of bacteria living in/on your body.

In Copenhagen, I live here and own three bikes. I co-organize a pop science event called Science and Cocktails which takes place in Christiania and aim to bring science and entertainment closer together by creating a series of public lectures intertwined with music/art performances and smoky dry-ice chilled cocktails.

I love bikes and like to learn more how to build/maintain them. I dream about a day when we all fly with our bikes.

I am not a vegeterian, but if I have a choice, I go with the vegeterian option. I like old movies, and once in a month organise a sci-fi movie night during the winter time, in one of the old audituriom.

At the beginning of my PhD, I funded Scalliom to bring microbial world into the social network, and organize social events based on people's apetitde. Some friends call it Fecebook.

In winter time, once a month, I organize a sci-fi movie-night in one of the old basement belong to the school where Lars Von Trier and others have used it in their movies.
